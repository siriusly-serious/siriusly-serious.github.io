Biography

   

About me...


Brandon Shen (b. 2006) is a composer, saxophonist, and pianist based in Loudoun County, VA. His influences include East Asian composers such as Joe Hisaishi and Takashi Yoshimatsu.

He has studied saxophone with renowned performers Billy Wolfe and Ed Fraedrich, piano with Billy Wolfe and Oxana Titova, and composition with Chris Evan Hass.
