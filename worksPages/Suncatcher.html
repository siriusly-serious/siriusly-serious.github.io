Suncatcher

(2026)

for soprano saxophone-glockenspiel performer, fixed media, and lights

I was thinking about how a sunbeam diffracts into a rainbow of colors and the simple joy of watching light shine and dance.

Suncatcher is written for (and is sort of a portrait of) my friend Quinlan Collins.

Lighting is optional. A version without glockenspiel is also available.
